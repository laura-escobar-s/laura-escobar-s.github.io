My name is Laura Escobar, a PhD student in the linguistics dept. at the University of Toronto. I work on Japanese sociophonetics, mediatized speech, and language and gender. In the department of Italian, Spanish and Portuguese I work with the bilinguals in Toronto lab on grammatical gender acquisition, heritage Spanish and vowel spaces.
